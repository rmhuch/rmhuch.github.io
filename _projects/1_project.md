In July of 2023, I joined the Short Period Planets Group (SuPerPiG)! Our goal is to collect accurate transit timings of Ultra-Hot Jupiter exoplanets over time in order to detect tiday decay, planets that are slowly being drawn into their host star. 

As a member of this group, I mentor undergraduate students in exoplanet transit observing and data analysis using python.
