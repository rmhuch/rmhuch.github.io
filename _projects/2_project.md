In 2023, our program was granted funding from NASA’s Science Activation Program to purchase telescopes to be distributed throughout classrooms in Idaho. As of September 2024, we have distributed 37 Unistellar eQuinox 2 telescopes and reached 45 teachers through our workshops. Learn more about our program at [boi.st/telescopes](boi.st/telescopes).
