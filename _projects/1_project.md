In July of 2023, I joined the Short Period Planets Group (SuPerPiG)! As a part of this group I led an exoplanetary transit observing campaign in the summer of 2023 and am now working on analuzing the data!

Our goal is to collect accurate transit timings of Ultra-Hot Jupiter exoplanets over time in order to detect tiday decay, planets that are slowly being drawn into their host star. 

We have a lot of exciting things in the work for this project, so check back soon for updates!
